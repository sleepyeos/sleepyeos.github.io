Gen128



A small code-128 bardode generator written in Python which is designed for embedding executable javascript into scannable barcodes. This utility can be used for exfiltration of sensitive data. It can also be used to launch a variation on an attack which is based on planting USB devices in public locations and waiting for an unsuspecting target to pick them up out of curiosity. Instead, the target may scan the barcode, at which point -- depending on the software used -- the embedded JS may immediately be executed in their browser.
