Translit Toolbar



A floating toolbar which allows users to "translit," or transliterate, text in real-time from the Latin alphabet (e.g. US/UK keyboards) into the Russian Cyrillic alphabet. This makes a dual-layout keyboard configuration unnecessary for those learning Russian or those who only need to type it ocassionally.
